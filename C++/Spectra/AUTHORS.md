- `<Spectra>/include/LinAlg/TridiagEigen.h`
- `<Spectra>/include/LinAlg/UpperHessenbergEigen.h`

- `Eigen/src/Eigenvaleus/SelfAdjointEigenSolver.h`

and Jitse Niesen <jitse@maths.leeds.ac.uk>.
